Bangladesh Tour

Spend a full day exploring the old capital of Bangladesh, the city of Sonargaon, with a guide. Learn about the ancient history of the country through a boat ride to a small island in the Meghna River, a visit to the Folk Arts & Crafts Museum, and the abandoned city of Panam, accompanied by in-depth commentary from your guide.
